01

What I will build

  • A clean, professional, mobile-first website for JM Bouwer Attorneys. Most people looking for a lawyer are on their phone, often in a stressful moment, so the site is built to make calling or messaging you the easiest thing on the screen.
  • One strong page (with room to grow into more) covering your five practice areas, an "about" section, and clear ways to get hold of you.
  • Built as a static website, hosted on GitHub. This keeps it fast, secure, and very cheap to run, with none of the constant updates or security holes that come with something like WordPress.
  • Prominent "Call now" and "WhatsApp" buttons, a short enquiry form that sends messages to your inbox, and your own branding throughout.
Please read — about your web address

The site will not show at jm-bouwerattorneys.co.za straight away

When the site is ready, it will first go live on a temporary GitHub web address so you can preview it and approve it. It will not appear at jm-bouwerattorneys.co.za until that domain's DNS settings are pointed at the new site.

To do that, I will need access to the domain's DNS settings, or for you to put me in touch with whoever currently manages the domain. Until that step is done, the .co.za address will keep showing the parked page it shows now. There is a question about this below.

03

The look and feel

  • Your own brand. I will use the burnt orange and deep indigo from your logo, on clean white. Everything will feel like one brand, in step with your marketing.
  • Serious and reassuring, not flashy. Plenty of space, clear headings, easy to read on a small screen.
  • Real over stock. Where possible I would rather use a photo of you and your office than generic stock images. People hire people, and it builds trust quickly.
  • Tidy copy. I will quietly correct a few small wording slips in the existing marketing (for example "prove" rather than "proof") without changing the meaning.
