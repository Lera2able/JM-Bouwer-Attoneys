About the Firm

Meet Julian Bouwer

Attorney · JM Bouwer Attorneys Inc.

JM Bouwer Attorneys Inc. is a Pretoria-based law firm built on a simple belief: every client deserves clear advice, honest guidance and an advocate who fights hard on their behalf.

Led by attorney Julian Bouwer, the firm represents clients across criminal, family, immigration and road accident matters, pairing sharp legal skill with a genuine commitment to the people it serves. Our doors are open to walk-in clients, and we pride ourselves on being approachable, responsive and straight with you from the first conversation.

“It is not only what you know, but what can be proven in court. We prepare every matter with that in mind.”
